Operating Challenges for Heavy Machines

Challenges operating heavy machines are varied according to the industries and environment they operate on, but could be generally listed as below:

Performance Deterioration

Gradual decline in machine efficiency and output quality over time

Unexpected Downtime

Unplanned interruptions that halt operations and reduce productivity

Inability to Complete Operational Plans

Failure to execute planned operations due to various constraints

Hazards at Work Environment

Environmental risks that threaten safe machine operation

Accidents

Unforeseen incidents that can cause damage and safety concerns
